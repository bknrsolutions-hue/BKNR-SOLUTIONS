Cookie Policy

Last updated: June 30, 2026

BKNR ERP uses standard cookies to ensure your session remains secure and active.

1. What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text assets stored on your terminal by your web browser. They contain secure tokens to map your local browser context to your active session.

2. Cookies We Use

  • Session Cookie (`bknr_session`): Holds your encrypted authentication state. Essential to access secure dashboards. Expires after 8 hours or upon logout.
  • Theme preference: Local storage setting to remember your Dark/Light mode choice.

3. Managing Cookies

You can configure your browser to disable or block cookies, but doing so will prevent you from authenticating or accessing the ERP portal.
