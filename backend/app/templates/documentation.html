Ecosystem Documentation

Welcome to the BKNR ERP documentation repository. This portal assists factory administrators and engineers in configuring scales, biometric shifts, and financial pipelines.

Ecosystem Setup

After receiving your activation Company ID, log into the gateway and complete the initial master configuration:

  • Create species entries (e.g. Vannamei, Black Tiger).
  • Configure sizing grades (e.g. 16/20, 21/25).
  • Register freezing chambers and plate setups.

Processing Operations

Operations track the workflow from raw material purchasing to block freezing:

Gate Entry -> RMP Weighing -> De-Heading -> Grading -> Peeling -> Soaking -> Production Freezer

Cold Vault Holding

All finished goods (FG) are stored under mapped batch codes. To record a stock entry:

  • Ensure batch cartons carry size-count markings.
  • Scan QR labels to assign location shelves.
