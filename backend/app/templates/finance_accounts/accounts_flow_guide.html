SVBK ERP Accounting Workflow

This guide explains the complete accounting workflow from source document entry to automatic ledger posting, outstanding management, and financial reporting. Enter each source document only once. The ERP automatically creates the required accounting entries, eliminating duplicate voucher entry.

Tally-style ERP Flow

End-to-End Diagram

1. Source Entry Enter the actual bill or invoice data in purchase, diesel, QA, freight, expense, and export invoice forms.
2. Auto Posting The ERP automatically generates the required accounting voucher. Manual duplicate voucher entry is not required.
3. Ledgers Accounts Payable, Accounts Receivable, Purchase, Sales, Expense, Inventory, GST, Cash & Bank, and General Ledger are updated.
4. Payment / Receipt Enter payments and receipts in Cash & Banking to clear outstanding balances.
5. Reports Transactions appear in dashboards, ledgers, outstanding reports, GST reports, P&L, balance sheet, cash book, and bank book.
Important: Customer Receivable, Vendor Payment, and Expense Voucher manual entry pages are intentionally hidden from normal users. Source documents automatically generate accounting vouchers, preventing duplicate postings and maintaining accounting accuracy.

English

Purchase / Packaging Bill

Entry: Enter vendor, invoice number, bill date, taxable amount, GST and total.

Reflects: Accounts Payable, Purchase Ledger, GST Reports and Profit & Loss.

Dr Purchase Account + Dr Input GST → Cr Vendor Payable

Diesel

Entry: Record diesel invoice purchases and daily consumption in the diesel page.

Reflects: Purchases increase diesel inventory; consumption moves inventory into fuel expense.

Purchase: Dr Diesel Inventory → Cr Vendor Payable. Consumption: Dr Fuel Expense → Cr Diesel Inventory

Export Commercial Invoice

Entry: Enter buyer, invoice no, currency, exchange rate and invoice value.

Reflects: Accounts Receivable and Export Sales are posted automatically.

Dr Accounts Receivable (Customer) → Cr Export Sales

Payment / Receipt

Entry: Select party ledger, bank/cash ledger, amount, date and reference.

Reflects: Outstanding gets cleared and bank/cash ledger is updated.

Vendor Payment: Dr Vendor Payable → Cr Bank/Cash. Customer Receipt: Dr Bank/Cash → Cr Customer Receivable

हिंदी

Purchase / Packaging Bill

Entry: Vendor name, invoice number, bill date, taxable amount, GST और total amount दर्ज करें.

Reflect: Accounts Payable, Purchase Ledger, GST Reports और P&L में दिखाई देगा.

Dr Purchase Account + Dr Input GST → Cr Vendor Payable

Diesel

Entry: Diesel purchase invoice और daily consumption को Diesel page में दर्ज करें.

Reflect: Purchase diesel inventory में जाएगा; consumption fuel expense में transfer होगा.

Purchase: Dr Diesel Inventory → Cr Vendor Payable. Consumption: Dr Fuel Expense → Cr Diesel Inventory

Export Commercial Invoice

Entry: Buyer, invoice number, currency, exchange rate और invoice value दर्ज करें.

Reflect: Accounts Receivable और Export Sales Ledger में automatic posting होगी.

Dr Accounts Receivable (Customer) → Cr Export Sales

Payment / Receipt

Entry: Party ledger, bank/cash ledger, amount, date और reference दर्ज करें.

Reflect: Outstanding clear होता है और bank/cash ledger update होता है.

Vendor Payment: Dr Vendor Payable → Cr Bank/Cash. Customer Receipt: Dr Bank/Cash → Cr Customer Receivable
