BKNR ERP

Next-Gen Intelligent Seafood Processing & Enterprise Ecosystem

BKNR ERP is a high-performance, industry-specific enterprise infrastructure engineered exclusively to automate and optimize seafood processing plants. By dissolving manual blind spots, the platform synchronizes complex factory-floor dynamics, supply chains, and fiscal metrics into a singular node of operational intelligence.

Operational Automation Matrix
  • Automated Gate Entry & Communications: Instant inbound weight logging integrated with real-time automated email alerts dispatched straight to management stacks upon vehicle entry.
  • Advanced HRMS & Smart Task Allocation: End-to-end workforce governance tracking attendance, shifts, and productivity with intelligent, constraint-based auto-work assignment to processing lines.
  • Live Inventory & Cold Storage Ecosystem: Dynamic balancing of floor stock, raw count variables, reprocess states, and automated lot positioning inside sub-zero bays to prevent batch mismatches.
  • Predictive Demand & Tracking: Real-time pending order tracking tied to factory capacities, showing raw material requirements automatically on the floor when a new trade order initializes.
  • Contractor Auditing & Billing: Algorithmic calculation of piece-rate peeling, de-heading, and sorting costs against contractor logs to generate finalized, audit-ready bills instantly.
Financial Governance & Analytics Intelligence
  • Running Balance Sheet & Ledger Streams: Unified chart of accounts tracking real-time operations with immediate ledger posting and deep debit/credit analysis for effortless funds-flow visibility.
  • Executive Performance Summery Nodes: Granular granular filters tracking yields, glaze distributions, and factory margins compiled into automated daily and monthly summary packets.
  • Multi-Dimensional Chart Analytics: Comprehensive visual intelligence reporting pipelines using structured analytical charts for tracking production cost variances at a single glance.
  • Isolation & Session Guardrails: Air-gapped multi-tenant architecture secured via strict session-wise data restrictions, guaranteeing distinct cross-company privacy boundaries.
