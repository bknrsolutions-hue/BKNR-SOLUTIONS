1. Multi-Company Command Architecture

Manage your entire corporate footprint from a single centralized database environment. Perfect for enterprise groups operating across multiple processing facilities or geographical branches.

  • Company-Wise Data Insulation: Global backend filtering layers automatically scope and restrict data matrices by authenticated firm payload, ensuring total multi-tenant security.
  • Isolated Enterprise Routing: Secure structural separation maps specific database queries dynamically per enterprise entity to avoid visibility overlap.
  • Role-Based Delegation: Empower managers with plant-specific credentials while maintaining complete executive access at the group level.
2. Real-Time Production & Transformation Intelligence

Gain absolute visibility into factory floor activities. The platform traces raw material transformations dynamically, leaving no space for unaccounted inventory leakage.

  • Dynamic Floor Balance Engines: Automated tracking arrays compute exact remaining floor balances continuously as stocks advance through processing lines.
  • HOSO to HLSO Conversion Analytics: Algorithmic transformation loops trace exact yield extractions, weight fluctuations, and de-heading metrics (e.g., Vannamei sizing).
  • Phase Optimization Metrics: Track efficiency rates across specialized processing stages including De-heading, Peeling, Soaking, and final Packing.
3. Smart Inventory & Cold Storage Asset Tracking

Protect your margins where it matters most. BKNR ERP provides deep visibility into cold holding spaces to ensure high turnover and asset security.

  • Holding Tables Integrity: Deep physical data architecture tracks batch allocations down to individual room placements and freezer maps.
  • Partial Dispatch Resolution: Sophisticated tracking models automatically manage partial dispatches, maintaining accurate continuous FIFO billing variables.
  • Aging Analysis: Monitor stock rotation timelines and automated storage expiration metrics to avoid product degradation.
4. Financial Costing & Profitability Engine

Eliminate estimation from your accounting. Our granular costing algorithms track the exact cost build-up for every processing batch.

  • Granular Process Billing: Automatically links raw material purchasing data, processing overheads, and contractor costs to calculate true net cost-per-kilogram.
  • Dual-Mode Storage Costing: Supports transitions between daily storage calculations and complex monthly bulk configurations.
  • Executive Performance Dashboards: Access real-time margin evaluations, pending sales requirements, and plant efficiency benchmarks instantaneously on page opening.
5. Real-Time HRMS & Manpower Intelligence

Maximize labor efficiency and control contractor costs with unified workforce intelligence explicitly integrated into production lines.

  • Live Manpower Tracking: Monitor active worker distribution and real-time headcounts directly on the production floor dynamically.
  • Contractor Billing Reconciliation: Automate contractor cost calculations by mapping piece-rate or shift attendance metrics directly against batch processing outputs.
  • Shift and Yield Correlation: Evaluate shift-wise productivity and labor expense parameters to pinpoint optimization opportunities.
6. High-Velocity Enterprise Interface

Engineered for corporate efficiency, minimizing operational friction and maximizing administrative entry speeds.

  • Intelligent searchable column mechanics across all dropdown tables for ultra-fast data selection.
  • Streamlined data-entry formats optimized for rapid corporate operational input.
  • Universal multi-device accessibility providing responsive interface performance across tablet, mobile, and desktop environments.
