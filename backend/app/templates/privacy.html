Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 30, 2026

BKNR ERP ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your company's data and biometric logs.

1. Information We Collect

We collect structural parameters and configuration parameters when you register an account, including:

  • Company Name, Address, and Contact details.
  • User credentials, names, mobile numbers, and roles.
  • Biometric attendance data logged on factory floors.
  • Production logs, cold storage records, and transactional ledger states.

2. Data Insulation & Security

Each tenant database segment is strictly isolated under a unique Company ID. This ensures that no data from your firm can be accessed or viewed by any other organization using the platform.

3. How We Use the Data

Collected logs are processed solely to drive system calculations, including salary processing, yield loss evaluations, inventory snapshots, and export document autofilling.

4. Contact Us

For security concerns or data removal requests, contact our compliance team at privacy@bknrsolutions.com.
